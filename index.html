My Story

Hello! I'm Rachel Rael, a Pediatric Occupational Therapist turned Data Analyst. My journey from healthcare to data analytics was born out of a desire for continual growth and transformation. Drawing from my background in Pediatric Occupational Therapy, I recognized a wealth of transferrable skills that seamlessly translated to the realm of data analytics.

My experience in problem-solving, empathetic communication, and holistic thinking laid a solid foundation, perfectly aligning with the analytical mindset required in the data field. As I embarked on this new path, I discovered that the puzzle-solving nature of my previous profession was akin to the detective work needed to uncover insights from data.

One of the driving forces behind my career switch was the promise of growth in data analytics. The dynamic landscape of data-driven decision-making offers boundless opportunities for development. From mastering technical tools to refining analytical techniques, I'm eager to thrive in an environment where learning is perpetual.

Just as in my previous role, collaboration remains integral to my approach. The transition from working in healthcare teams to collaborating with diverse experts in data projects feels natural. The camaraderie, brainstorming, and collective pursuit of innovative solutions propel my enthusiasm for this new chapter.

In the heart of this career transition lies my unwavering commitment to embracing change, adapting to challenges, and continuously evolving. I'm excited to fuse the insights gained from my past with the potentials of the future to navigate the world of data analytics with passion and purpose.
